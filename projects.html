I do a lot on the side.

  • QuickCite
    If you have a high-school education you’ve probably written a paper or two, and been subjected to the tedious task of citing sources for that paper. QuickCite minimizes this tedium. Now all you do is scan the barcode on your source with your iPhone/Android, and we send a properly-formatted citation to your inbox.
  • Minehub
    Played Minecraft? Then you know it’s awesome. Minehub lets you play minecraft with your friends easily with on-demand servers. We’re currently in private beta.
  • Lifetracker
    Lifetracker is my current favourite project. It is a system that lets you track arbitrary data about your life using your phone, and then finds interesting correlations you can use to live smarter and better.
  • Ampersand
    Ampersand was a weekend project of mine and Liam Miller-Cushon. We piped a souped-up node-chat through the Google Translate API and ended up with Universal Translator Chat!
  • 7Cubed
    Sometimes school just doesn’t scratch the creative itch, that’s why last November, myself and six of the best hackers I know locked ourselves in an office and tried to write a complete software product every day for a week. We called it 7Cubed: 7 Hackers 7 Apps 7Days.
